WHO WE ARE

About AppMint

AppMint specializes in autonomous drone systems, computer vision, IoT automation, and custom software platforms.

We design and develop complete end-to-end solutions — from embedded systems and firmware customization to AI-powered detection systems, secure flight control software, and scalable cloud-connected applications.

Our expertise includes PX4, ArduPilot, MAVLink, NVIDIA Jetson, Raspberry Pi, real-time AI inference, swarm coordination, firmware security, and custom Ground Control Station (GCS) development.

We transform complex engineering challenges into reliable, production-ready systems.
